About us:

Manila Tytana Colleges (Tytana), the educational arm of the Metrobank Group, has carried on a tradition of academic excellence since 1975. Transforming generations of students into professionals with a strong sense of work ethics, it continues to break new grounds to stay ahead in an ever-changing world. Tytana embarks on a new vision of being a model institution of higher learning, producing scholars and professionals for national and global endeavors through values-driven progressive education founded on the culture of excellence and nurturance. Tytana honors its commitment to provide a broad and liberal education, to develop the potential of each student, to guide them towards the attainment of their goals, and to prepare them to become responsive to the needs of society.

Over the years, Tytana has been branded for excellence in nursing education and other fields such as psychology, nutrition with culinary arts, tourism and hospitality management, information technology, accountancy, entrepreneurship, business administration, and communication.
